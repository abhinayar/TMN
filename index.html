Founded in January 2014 out of Yale University, Trinity Mobile Networks has pioneered the first and only Endpoint SDN platform, Jumpnet, to integrate all the world’s networks. Whether it is cellular, WiFi, mesh, bluetooth, satellite, or a future connection standard, Jumpnet works to make connectivity as easy and inexpensive as possible.

Jumpnet is a software-only OTT solution designed to run on iOS, Android, and most other networked devices. Through network unification and universal compatibility, we believe we can keep access to the internet open and affordable.
